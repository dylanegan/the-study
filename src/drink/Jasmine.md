# Jasmine

* **45 ml** gin
* **15 ml** *Cointreau*
* **10 ml** *Campari*
* **15 ml** lemon juice
* Lemon twist

## Variations

### *The Savoy Cocktail Book*

* **45 ml** gin
* **7.5 ml** *Cointreau*
* **7.5 ml** *Campari*
* **22.5 ml** lemon juice

Shake with cracked ice until very cold, strain into chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a lemon twist. Pucker up.
