# Cable Car

* **60 ml** spiced rum ( *Kraken* )
* **30 ml** triple sec or orange curacao
* **15 ml** lemon juice
* **7.5 ml** 2:1 simple syrup
* **1/2** egg white

Shake and fine strain into chilled coupe.
