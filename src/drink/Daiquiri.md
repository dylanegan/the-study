# Daiquiri

## Variations

### IBA

* **45 ml** white rum
* **25 ml** lime juice
* **15 ml** simple syrup

Shake and strain into a cocktail glass.

### *Daiquiri Natural No.1 #1*

* **2.5 shot** white rum ( *Bacardi Superior* )
* **0.75 shot** lime juice
* **0.5 shot** simple syrup
